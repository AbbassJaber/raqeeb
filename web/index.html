Environmental monitoring · Lebanon

RAQEEB watches Lebanon from space

An AI agent scans Sentinel-2 satellite imagery for likely environmental violations — illegal quarrying and coastal encroachment — and reasons about whether each change breaks a rule.

  1. Pick a candidate on the map or list — or draw a box / ask Raqeeb to scan a new area.
  2. Watch the agent analyze it live: detect change → classify with vision → check the law.
  3. Review the evidence and prepare an alert for a human reviewer.

Every result is a candidate for human verification — never an accusation. Boundaries are proxies; alerts are drafted for a person to send, never automatically.
